★ Our Manifesto

Five Demands. Non-Negotiable.

We do not negotiate with systems that were never designed to listen. These are not requests — they are demands.

01

Accountability, Not Apology Tours

Officials who fail citizens must face real consequences — not press conferences where they express "deep regret" and carry on unchanged.

02

Transparent Public Spending

Every rupee of public money must be traceable, published, and legible to the people who funded it. No more invisible budgets for visible crises.

03

Education That Actually Educates

A curriculum designed for curiosity, not rote memorisation. A system that produces thinkers, not paper-passers.

04

Healthcare as a Right, Not a Privilege

No citizen should choose between debt and survival. Basic healthcare belongs to everyone — not just those who can afford the premium.

05

An End to Performance Governance

We have had enough of ministers who are excellent at appearing to govern. We demand people who are actually good at it.

★ Eligibility

Who Can Join?

Membership in the Cockroach Citizen Party is open to anyone who meets the following rigorous criteria:

✓
You have been ignored by at least one government policy that directly affected you.
✓
You are capable of feeling frustrated at a system that should — and does not — work.
✓
You still believe, stubbornly, that things can be better.
✓
You are willing to keep showing up, like a cockroach, no matter how many times the system tries to swat you down.

No application fee. No membership dues. No corporate endorsement required. Just a pulse and a grievance.
